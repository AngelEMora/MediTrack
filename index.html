Precise control, safe results

Innovative platform designed to optimize and manage all aspects related to operating room administration and medical inventory management. Our comprehensive solution ranges from surgery scheduling and resource allocation to postoperative patient monitoring, guaranteeing efficiency and precision at every stage of the surgical process.

Our Services

What We Provide

  • Inventory Management

    MediTrack allows detailed recording of drugs and surgical materials, including batch control, expiration dates and available quantities.

  • Scheduling of Surgical Interventions

    Advanced tools to assign operating rooms, medical staff and resources needed for each procedure, ensuring everything is ready and available when needed.

  • Postoperative Follow-up

    It facilitates the recording and monitoring of patient recovery, documenting complications and significant improvements, and generating detailed reports for follow-up.

  • Alerts and Notifications

    Alert system to reorder medications, notifications about drugs about to expire, and reminders for periodic inventory audits.

  • Reports

    Generation and export of reports in various formats to facilitate the analysis and distribution of information.

  • Operating Efficiency

    Automate critical processes, reduce paper use and minimize human errors.
